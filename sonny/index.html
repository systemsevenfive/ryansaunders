Sonny

My personal knowledge tool.

Status● Tinkering
Year2026
PlatformWeb · Vanilla JS
TrackEXP-005 · AI

01Why it exists

Two things at once. First, my personal tech-term glossary: concepts I want to keep fresh, with the relationships between them visible at a glance. Second, a prototype for what I really want: a tool that ingests an unfamiliar iOS codebase and shows me its major structures and how they connect, so I can ramp up in hours instead of weeks.

02What I'm exploring

The interesting design work is the contract between Sonny (the renderer) and any generator that produces a model: a small, versioned JSON schema with categories, nodes, and typed relationships. Hand-authored mental maps today. A code analyzer tomorrow. The renderer doesn't care which.

Vanilla JS, no build step. Cytoscape.js handles the layout. The complexity lives in the schema, not the framework choice.

A clean contract beats a clever framework. Make the data shape obvious and the rest gets easier.
Vanilla JS Cytoscape.js JSON Schema Graph Visualization

03What's next

A generator that walks an iOS project (Swift modules, types, view-model relationships, dependency edges) and emits a Sonny model. The renderer is the easy part. Making the generator opinionated enough to be useful, and conservative enough to be honest, is the work.
