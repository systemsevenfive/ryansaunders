Chartula

Business cards remembered, mapped, and not lost in a drawer.

Status● Tinkering
Year2026
PlatformiOS 26 · SwiftUI
TrackEXP-004 · Tool

01Why it exists

I keep meeting people, taking their card, and then losing track of who they were and where I met them. Chartula is the answer to "I know I have that person's card somewhere".

02What I'm exploring

Snap a card, OCR the contents, drop a pin where you met. Searchable later by company, name, or location. The interesting design question is how to make it feel like a tool, not yet another contact app.

The card is the artefact. The memory of where you got it is the actual value.
SwiftUI SwiftData Vision MapKit Foundation Models MVVM

03What's next

Get the capture flow under five seconds. If it's slower than just keeping the physical card, the app fails.
