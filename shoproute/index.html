ShopRoute

Routing your weekly shop to save money, not just steps.

Status● Prototype
Year2026
PlatformiOS 26 · SwiftUI
TrackEXP-002 · ML

01Why it exists

Most people I know shop at the same handful of grocery stores within a few kilometres of home. The same items have wildly different prices week to week across those stores, and nobody has the patience to comparison shop manually.

02What I'm exploring

You give it your usual list. It learns the prices it sees over time at the stores you actually visit, then tells you the cheapest split for this week's run, and what's worth a detour versus what isn't.

A small, polite optimization for the boring economics of feeding a household.
SwiftUI SwiftData Foundation Models MapKit Core Location MVVM

03What's next

A working manual-entry version first. Anything fancier (receipt scanning, flyer ingestion) is a v2 problem.
