Solitaire Zero

Classic Klondike Solitaire, quietly polished. Free, no accounts, no ads, no data collection — just the game.

Status● Shipping soon
Year2026
PlatformiOS 26 · SwiftUI
Next upApp Store launch

01Why it exists

Most mobile solitaire apps are loud. They want your attention, your email, your permissions, your money. Solitaire Zero is the quiet alternative — a solitaire app that respects your time and your device. Free. No accounts. No ads. No tracking. Just the game.

02What I figured out

The game itself is trivial to build. The hard part is restraint — every time a temptation appears to "add value" (leaderboards, streaks, daily challenges), ask whether it makes solitaire feel more like solitaire or less. The answer is almost always less.

Technically: a hand-rolled SwiftUI card layout with physics-driven drag interactions, adaptive layouts for iPhone and iPad, and a one-tap "new game" experience that respects muscle memory.

Solitaire is a meditation, not a slot machine. The app should feel the same.
SwiftUI SwiftData iOS 26 MVVM

03What's next

Launch on the App Store. After that: Vegas scoring, an iPad-specific layout, and maybe — maybe — a second game mode. No promises.
