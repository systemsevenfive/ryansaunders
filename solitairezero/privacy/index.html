Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

This policy describes how Solitaire Zero (“the app”) handles information. The app is provided by Ryan Saunders (Ontario, Canada).

Summary

Solitaire Zero does not require special permissions, does not ask you to create an account, and does not collect personal data for analytics, advertising, or tracking. The app is designed to run entirely on your device.

Information we do not collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or other contact details
  • Precise location or other sensitive device permissions
  • Analytics, crash reports tied to your identity, or advertising identifiers
  • Any data for sale or sharing with data brokers

Local data on your device

Like many games, the app may store ordinary settings or in-progress game state (for example, to remember a deal or preferences) using storage that stays on your device. That information is not sent to us and is not accessible to us.

Children

The app is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, we will update this page and the “Last updated” date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy or the app? You can reach out through the contact options on the main site.
