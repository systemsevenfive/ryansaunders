02How AI fits in

It's everywhere, it's changing quickly, and every day brings new workflows and skills to learn. As companies embrace AI, ensuring quality output from the "raw material" is paramount. It's not enough to let a swarm of agents build your app from scratch. Good software practices are more important than ever with an army of tireless, willing, and incredibly literal agents at our disposal.

I tinker with a multi-agent workflow. Tickets live in Nexus, with acceptance criteria clearly defined. Visual and architectural designs are agreed and recorded for agent reference. Guardrails are in place to determine when to let an agent merge work and when human review is required. Testing is handled by combination of agent and human. Test, review, iterate.
